ELECSTA 2K24

A National Level Technical Symposium

ELECSTA 2k24 is here !

ELECSTA from the Electronics and Communication Department of Government College of Engineering, Dharmapuri is a national level symposium to be held on 4th April consists of a series of technical events for acquiring knowledge and other fun activities to make the stay memorable. It is completely organized by a team of dedicated students to manifest the spirit of students of Electronics and Communication Engineering and enable the participants to showcase their talents. But that’s not all! We’ve got exciting prizes for the winners.

Register now and join us to interact with the technical ideas and build your knowledge with lots of fun.

About us

Welcome to Elecsta 24!

ELECSTA 2K24 is a National Level Event which is one of the proud venture of our Association of Electronics and Communication Engineering. It is our immense pleasure to invite each and every unique mind with magnificent ideas longing for a stage to exhibit out. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, an artist, a gamer, or a performer, Elecsta 24 has something for everyone. Join us as we celebrate the diverse tapestry of talent and creativity, inspire collaboration and exploration, and create unforgettable memories together.

Tech-Quiz

This event will be a online program so bring your mobile. It consist of two parts:
Round 1 : General IQ questions.
Round 2 : Electronics Related Questions.
Syllabus: Digital Electronics,Analog Circuits,Digital Communication,Signals and Systems.
Innovation, precision and technical creativity is appreciated.

Coding Master

X

Coding Master

This event will be on laptop/System. Systems will be provided here no need to bring your own laptop.It consists of two part:
Round 1 : Patterns & String Manipulation.
Round 2 : Solving a problem statement.
Each round is an elimination round.
Languages: C , C++ , Java , Python. Any of these languages can be used
Innovation, precision and technical creativity is appreciated.
Note: The decision of Event Coordinators will be final.

Circuit Debugging

This event is a Hands-on activity. This event consists of two Rounds:
Round 1 : To solve the given circuit with minimal mistakes.
Round 2 : A circuit will be shown for 2 minutes and be asked to replicate the same circuit on your own.
Innovation, precision and technical creativity is appreciated.
Note: The decision of Event Coordinators will be final.

JAM

X

JAM

The event name is abbreviation for “Just A Minute”. This event has only one round, Where the participant will be given a on spot topic to speak for a minute.
Rules: The participant will be given a chance to search and gather knowledge about the topic for 2 mins. After 2mins a timer will run for 1 minute to present on the topic.
Syllabus: The current affair on Electronics Industry & Core subject Knowledge testing Topics.
Innovation, Communication Skills and technical creativity is appreciated.
NOTE: The decision of Judges will be final.
